Eclipse (2025)

This exquisite piece, commissioned by André for his beloved wife, Cindy, is a celebration of elegance and artistic innovation. The double-layered dress exudes a sense of mystery and allure with its semi-transparent fabric, creating a mesmerizing moiré effect as the layers interact. The subtle interplay of light and texture lends the garment an almost hypnotic quality, captivating the eye and drawing one into its intricate design.

The shoulders are adorned with delicate ribbons of fine strings, cascading gracefully to add a soft yet dynamic movement to the overall silhouette. These strings serve as both a functional and aesthetic feature, framing the shoulders and complementing the dress's flowing form. The combination of modern design techniques and timeless sophistication makes this dress not only a striking visual statement but also a deeply personal gift, embodying André's admiration for Cindy.
